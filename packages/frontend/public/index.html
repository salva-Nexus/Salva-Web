SALVA

On-Chain Payment Protocol for Nigeria

Salva is Nigeria's on-chain financial protocol built on Base L2 (Ethereum Layer 2). It maps human-readable account aliases to crypto wallet addresses — so you send payments to an account alias like alice@salva or a 10-digit account number, not a long hexadecimal wallet address.

Salva accounts are powered by Safe smart wallets — no seed phrases needed, zero gas fees for users, and instant NGN-denominated settlement on-chain.

How it works

  • Create a Salva account — get a unique human-readable alias and account number
  • Your alias maps to a smart wallet address on Base blockchain
  • Send and receive NGNs (Nigerian Naira stablecoin) instantly
  • No seed phrases. No gas fees. No long wallet addresses.
  • Multiple apps can each have their own isolated namespace — @salva, @coinbase, @uniswap

Why Salva

Crypto payments today require copying and pasting 42-character wallet addresses. Salva solves this by creating a permanent, phishing-resistant on-chain registry that links your identity to your wallet — the same way DNS links domain names to IP addresses.
